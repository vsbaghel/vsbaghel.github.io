Dr. Vivek Singh Baghel

Assistant Professor
Department of Computer Science & Engineering,
Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology, Jais, Amethi-229304, India.

       

Currently, I am working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, RGIPT, Jais, Amethi, India. Prior to that, I worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Intelligent Control lab under the supervision of Prof. Leehter Yao at Taipei Tech, Taiwan for a year.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science & Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Indore, India in March 2023, and my M.Tech. in Information Technology from the University of Hyderabad, Hyderabad, India, in 2018. During my PhD, I worked in the Pattern Analysis & Machine Intelligence lab under the supervision of Dr. Surya Prakash. In my PhD thesis work, I worked in the area of biometric template protection which includes the development of secure and efficient techniques to protect the fingerprint user template stored in the database of a biometric authentication system.

Research Interests:

  • Computer Vision
  • Biometrics
  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
  • Social Network Analysis
